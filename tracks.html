Competition Tracks

Four Disciplines

Each track represents a pillar of the Meridian Challenge. Participants are grouped across all four to form balanced, interdisciplinary teams.

I

Finance

المالية

Get ready to experience the future of global finance through an innovative combination of finance and strategy. Network with leading financial professionals in areas such as capital markets, fintech, risk management, and the global banking system.

This track will challenge you to explore new ways of viewing financial systems and developing resilient, interconnected economies by applying a variety of approaches to various components of the global economy. Learn directly from senior industry leaders, top investment banks, and the world's best finance professors.

Through competition, you will develop and demonstrate your ability to construct bold business models; create and execute complex capital structures; analyse real-life risks; and develop and present business cases that will withstand scrutiny at the international level. This is a unique opportunity for you to demonstrate your skills, be a leader, and help shape the future of the global economy.

Key Topics

Capital Markets Fintech & DeFi Risk Modelling ESG & Impact Investing Sovereign Wealth Islamic Finance Venture Capital Quantitative Analysis

II

Law

القانون

Step into the fast-paced world where law is the engine of global commerce, cutting-edge technology and critical infrastructure. Participants will gain a background in corporate law, arbitration, intellectual property and regulatory affairs — becoming the architects of legally sound, game-changing business solutions that will bring fresh perspectives to the next generation of this industry.

From the lens of the Meridian Challenge, we pose high-stakes scenarios to legal enthusiasts, including navigating complex regulations across borders, ensuring airtight compliance and developing unshakeable governance frameworks. In addition, they must also defend their proposals to an elite panel of judges under intense scrutiny.

Workshops led by top-tier practitioners from premier international law firms and leading legal academics provide participants with rare access to the strategies used to shape global business law.

Key Topics

International Arbitration Regulatory Compliance Intellectual Property Corporate Governance Commercial Law Data Privacy & AI Ethics Cross-Border Litigation Emerging Market Regulation

III

Engineering

الهندسة

The Engineering Track challenges participants to create, test, and bring to market new and innovative solutions for some of the biggest systemic problems of the current time. From sustainable urban development and intelligent infrastructure to AI, advanced manufacturing and many others, engineers are turning bold concepts into tangible results.

You and your team are creating a centre for technology-driven innovation within the Meridian Challenge, creating viable, scalable solutions and clearly and confidently communicating complex ideas to a panel of leading experts in their fields.

Workshops feature leaders from world-class engineering firms, smart city development authorities, and university research labs at the cutting edge of sustainable technology and systems design.

Key Topics

Sustainable Infrastructure AI & Machine Learning Smart City Design Systems Engineering Renewable Energy Robotics & Automation Advanced Manufacturing Water & Resource Management

IV

Management

الإدارة

The Management track challenges participants to translate ambition into execution. Drawing on disciplines from organisational behaviour and strategic leadership to operations management and entrepreneurship, management specialists bring the operational and strategic scaffolding that determines whether ideas succeed or collapse at a real scale.

In the Meridian Challenge, management participants lead team coordination, define organisational structures, manage resource allocation, and present cohesive business strategies to a judging panel of senior executives. They are responsible for ensuring that the financial model, legal framework, and engineering solution form a unified, deliverable proposal.

Workshops are led by C-suite executives, MBA faculty from leading business schools, and entrepreneurs who have scaled ventures across the Gulf and beyond. Sessions cover strategic decision-making under uncertainty, stakeholder management, and building organisations that endure.

Key Topics

Strategic Leadership Operations & Execution Organisational Design Entrepreneurship & Growth Corporate Strategy Stakeholder Management Change Management Business Model Innovation

The Meridian Challenge

How Teams Are Formed

Each team in the Meridian Challenge is composed of participants from all four tracks — finance, law, engineering, and management. Teams are strategically assembled to ensure a balance of expertise, creating a microcosm of the real-world collaboration needed to solve complex, multi-dimensional problems.

Teams will face a series of escalating challenges: initial problem analysis, solution design, crisis pivots, and final presentations. Judges evaluate not only the quality of the solution but the team's ability to integrate perspectives, communicate across disciplines, and perform under pressure.

"The Meridian is not where one discipline ends — it is where they all converge."
