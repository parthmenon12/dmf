البرنامج

Programme

Five-Day Schedule

A meticulously structured programme balancing academic rigour, competitive intensity, and prestigious social engagements.

Legend: Ceremony Social Workshop Competition Panel Brunch Networking

Day 1

Opening & Welcome

اليوم الأول

Opening Ceremony

Welcome address by the DMF Chairman, keynote from an international dignitary, and the formal inauguration of the 2026 Dubai Meridian Forum.

Ceremony 09:00 – 11:30

Team Formation & Orientation

Participants are assigned to interdisciplinary teams. Meet your teammates, receive the Meridian Challenge briefing, and begin strategic planning.

Competition 12:00 – 14:30

Opening Gala Dinner

A black-tie evening reception with curated dining, live entertainment, and introductory networking among participants, judges, and distinguished guests.

Social 19:00 – 23:00

Day 2

Workshops & Round 1

اليوم الثاني

Morning Brunch

An elegant morning spread with continental and Arabic cuisine. Informal networking over breakfast.

Brunch 08:00 – 09:30

Track-Specific Workshops — Session A

Parallel workshops across Finance (Capital Markets & Risk), Law (Regulatory Frameworks), and Engineering (Systems Design & Sustainability).

Workshop 10:00 – 12:30

Meridian Challenge — Round 1

Teams receive their first problem set. Initial analysis, strategy formulation, and preliminary pitch preparation under time pressure.

Competition 14:00 – 17:30

Industry Leaders Panel

Moderated discussion featuring senior executives from across the three tracks on the theme of interdisciplinary innovation in the Gulf region.

Panel 18:30 – 20:00

Day 3

Deep Dives & Crisis Round

اليوم الثالث

Executive Brunch

Hosted brunch with invited judges and industry leaders for informal mentorship and conversation.

Brunch 08:00 – 09:30

Track-Specific Workshops — Session B

Advanced sessions: Finance (Fintech & DeFi), Law (IP & Data Privacy), Engineering (AI & Automation). Interactive case-study format.

Workshop 10:00 – 12:30

Meridian Challenge — Crisis Pivot Round

Teams are hit with an unexpected crisis scenario. They must adapt their solutions in real time, demonstrating agility, leadership, and cross-functional coordination.

Competition 14:00 – 18:00

Judges' Fireside Chat

An intimate evening session where judges share career insights and answer participant questions on leadership, innovation, and the future of their fields.

Panel 19:00 – 20:30

Day 4

Final Preparations & Semifinals

اليوم الرابع

Networking Brunch

Final brunch with themed discussion tables focusing on career pathways in finance, law, and engineering.

Brunch 08:00 – 09:30

Track-Specific Workshops — Session C

Final workshop round: Finance (ESG & Impact), Law (Cross-Border Litigation), Engineering (Renewable Energy & Smart Cities). Practical application focus.

Workshop 10:00 – 12:00

Meridian Challenge — Semifinal Presentations

Teams present refined pitches to the full judging panel. Adversarial Q&A, stress-testing of business models, and technical scrutiny determine finalists.

Competition 13:30 – 18:00

Finalist Announcement & Evening Reception

Announcement of teams advancing to the Grand Final. Celebratory cocktail reception with live music.

Social 19:30 – 22:00

Day 5

Grand Final & Closing

اليوم الخامس

Grand Final — Meridian Challenge

Finalist teams deliver their ultimate presentations before the complete judging panel and a live audience. The defining moment of the forum.

Competition 09:30 – 13:00

Awards Ceremony

Formal presentation of the Meridian Award, track-specific honours, and special recognitions. Keynote address by the Chief Judge.

Ceremony 15:00 – 17:00

Closing Gala & Networking Dinner

The culminating event of DMF 2026 — a prestigious black-tie gala dinner celebrating achievement, forging lasting connections, and closing the forum in style.

Social 19:30 – Late
