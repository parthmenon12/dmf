August 2 – 5, 2026  ·  Business Bay, Dubai

Programme Schedule

Four days of structured intensity — from expert masterclasses and competitive pitching to a series of surprises on Day 3, culminating in final presentations and a formal closing ceremony.

Day One
The Masterclass Bootcamp & Opening Gala
Sunday, 2 August 2026

اليوم الأول

07:00 – 08:00
Arrival
Registration & Arrival
Delegates collect credentials, meet their assigned teams, and take their places in the main hall.
08:00 – 09:00
Ceremony
Opening Ceremony
Formal inauguration of the Dubai Meridian Forum 2026, with welcome addresses and key announcements.
09:00 – 10:00
Panel
Industry Expert Panel
The judging panel opens a high-level discourse on the state of business, innovation, and industry — setting the tone for the days ahead.
— Short break —
10:00 – 16:00
Masterclass
Four Masterclass Modules
An intensive educational programme covering the four core disciplines of the Forum. Taught directly by the judging panel, these sessions equip every delegate with the foundational knowledge required to compete. Details are disclosed on the day.
— Recess · Delegates prepare for the evening —
19:30 – 21:30
Gala
The Dubai Meridian Gala Dinner
A formal, catered evening event. All delegates, the judging panel, VIP sponsors, and partner representatives gather for a prestige networking dinner — the first major high-level networking opportunity of the Forum.
Day Two
The Gauntlet
Monday, 3 August 2026

اليوم الثاني

06:00 – 08:00
Activity
Morning Group Activity (optional)
An early-morning physical activity designed to build team cohesion and energy before a full day of presentations.
09:30 – 10:00
Prep
Morning Registration & Preparation
Teams settle in, load materials, and complete final preparation before presentations commence.
10:00 – 13:00
Competition
Presentations — Morning Block
The first group of teams present to the judging panel. Teams not on stage remain active throughout the day.
— Lunch break · 13:00 – 14:00 —
14:00 – 17:00
Competition
Presentations — Afternoon Block
The remaining teams complete their presentations. All delegates participate in the programme throughout.
17:00 – 17:45
Panel
Judges' Address & Observations
The panel addresses the full room, sharing candid observations from the day's presentations. A valuable collective learning moment.
Day Three
The Turning Point
Tuesday, 4 August 2026

اليوم الثالث

09:30 – 10:00
Prep
Morning Registration & Preparation
Doors open. Delegates settle in and prepare for what will be the most demanding day of the Forum.
10:00 – 14:00
Undisclosed
To Be Revealed on the Day
The morning programme on Day 3 is intentionally undisclosed. What happens in this window will be one of the defining moments of the Forum. Come prepared for anything.
— Lunch —
15:30 – 17:45
Panel
Expert Panel & Open Discourse
Selected delegates join industry experts and judges on stage for a live, dialogue-based discussion — a dynamic alternative to a traditional defence format.
18:00 – 19:00
Debrief
End-of-Day Address
Judges close the day with a forward-looking address, congratulating delegates and setting expectations for the Final Defense.
Day Four
The Final Defense
Wednesday, 5 August 2026

اليوم الرابع

09:30 – 10:00
Prep
Morning Registration & Preparation
Final logistics and AV checks. Delegates arrive and prepare for the closing day of competition.
10:00 – 13:00
Competition
Final Presentations — Part I
Teams deliver their final, polished pitch to the judging panel. The first group presents in the morning session.
— Lunch break · 13:00 – 14:00 —
14:00 – 15:30
Competition
Final Presentations — Part II
The remaining teams complete their final pitches. Upon the last presentation, the competitive programme of the Forum is officially closed.
15:30 – 17:30
Networking
VIP Networking & Private Meetings
An exclusive networking session with sponsors, investors, and partners. Select delegates access private one-on-one meetings during this window.
17:30 – 18:30
Ceremony
Closing Ceremony & Announcements
The Chief Organisers formally close the Dubai Meridian Forum 2026. Shortlisted teams are announced on stage. The path forward — for investment, placements, and partnerships — is revealed.

The schedule above reflects the confirmed programme structure. Specific session details, challenge content, and supplementary activities are intentionally withheld and will be disclosed to registered delegates in advance of the event.
